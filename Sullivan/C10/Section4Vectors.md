__44__ \& __46__) Given $\mathbf{v} = 3\mathbf{i} - 5\mathbf{j}, \mathbf{w} = -2\mathbf{i} + 3\mathbf{j}$, calculate:

__44__) $\|\mathbf{v} + \mathbf{w}\|$

__Sln__: $\|\mathbf{v} + \mathbf{w}\| = \|(3-2)\mathbf{i} + (-5+3)\mathbf{j}\| = \|1\mathbf{i}-2\mathbf{j}\| = \sqrt{1^2 + (-2)^2} = \boxed{\sqrt5} \doteq 2.236.$
